Do you offer individual lessons?
No, and for good reasons, it sounds counter intuitive but students do better in group lessons becaus...
  • Your child must learn to thrive in an Exam style setting. Learning in small groups mirrors that environment, helping children develop focus and pace under light pressure.
  • Motivation through peer learning – Working alongside other children builds healthy competition and helps students stay motivated. Seeing others tackle similar challenges boosts confidence and effort.
  • Collaborative learning is essential – Children often learn by hearing others’ answers or approaches. Discussing reasoning and strategies reinforces understanding and exposes them to different problem-solving methods.
  • More affordable – Group lessons usually cost less per hour than individual tuition, providing great value without compromising on quality.
